The Dark Side of AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing the world, but it also has some concerning drawbacks. First, AI is replacing human jobs, especially in fields like customer support, manufacturing, and data entry. Second, it can sometimes produce biased or false information, leading to dangerous consequences. Moreover, excessive use of AI threatens personal privacy as data is collected and analyzed. Therefore, while AI is powerful, we must use it responsibly and create strong policies to reduce its risks.
