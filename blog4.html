FireLens (Fire-Boltt): From Smartwatches to AI Eyewear — A New Chapter in Wearables

Published: September 27, 2025

Genesis & Founders’ Backstory

Fire-Boltt began as a wearable tech company in India, focusing initially on smartwatches, fitness wearables, and audio accessories. The company was co-founded by Arnav Kishore and his sister Aayushi Kishore. Arnav often plays the public role of CEO and brand face, while Aayushi has been involved in operations, product strategy, and design orientation.

Education & Background

Aayushi holds a Master’s in Economics from Delhi University and an Executive MBA from Harvard Business School. Arnav attended Pathways World School before diving into entrepreneurship.

Evolution into Smart Eyewear — FireLens

In September 2025, Fire-Boltt launched FireLens, India’s first AI-powered smart eyewear line. Product variants include FireLens Audio and FireLens Vision AI, offering features like 8MP camera, real-time translation, object recognition, and more.

Growth, Position & Challenges

Fire-Boltt had a strong foothold with millions of smartwatch sales. FireLens aims for a premium category; challenges include battery, privacy, AI accuracy and after-sales support.
